# Devops
DevOps is fundamentally about unifying teams and fostering a culture of continuous improvement, ensuring that obstacles are removed in the pursuit of delivering quality software quickly.
## What is Not DevOps
- Not a Title like DevOps Engineer 
- Not an End to End framework for software development
- [DevOps Anti Types](https://web.devopstopologies.com/)

## Culture Considerations
- Know the WHY of your company 
- Continual Improvement is needed to adapt to ongoing world (People, Process and Technology)
